Campus Life

School Church

The final week of the Autumn Term included the School’s Carol Service, held on Wednesday 11 December in the stunning setting of the Cathedral and Abbey Church of St Alban, when parents, Old Albanians and guests were treated to a spectacular evening. Hymns included Once in Royal David’s City and Tell Out, My Soul as well as In the Bleak Mid-winter and Hark! The Herald-Angels Sing. Readings were given by pupils including the Head of School and Roger Fletcher, OA. The choir treated attendees to stunning renditions of The Truth From Above, A Spotless Rose and Advent ‘O’ Carol. The Service proved to be another memorable event for all present.

School Library

This library is quite large with many rooms, includig the junior library for the first 2 year groups, 2 computer suites and a large amount of hal space for people who need silence. Fictional and books for young teens are in the junior library and other books, including Economics and History.



School Sport Field

On site the Old Albanian Club House has 8 changing rooms, referee changing rooms and treatment rooms. We have 2 bars with a large upstairs balconies overlooking the 1st XV pitch (Pitch 1) in one direction and the 1st XI Cricket Pitch & Rugby Training Pitch (Pitch 5) in the other.
